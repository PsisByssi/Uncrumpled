Profile
A way to group and organise workbooks and notes. This is considered the top tier. You can rewrite your entire settings by having a different profile.
Page
A Page is created using the rules defined in the workbook, as well as the Profile setting. You can think of a page as the file to a word processing program. Each time you want to store new information, you need a page for that.
Work book
A Collection of Notes, A workbook is what you connect with a hotkey. Generally a workbook always displays Notes of the same widget type.
Work book archetype
Workbooks come in different forms, these are refereed to as archetypes or widgets. A Workbook may be a general note taking page or a full blown project manager.

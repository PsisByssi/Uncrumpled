Notes¶

The Note Archetype is very simple to use. It provides you with a simple text editor.

Supported Features¶

Text editing behavior modeled from Open office.

  • Changing font style by selecting the text and then e.g Ctrl + B
  • Changing font style by clicking in the middle of a word and e.g Ctrl + B

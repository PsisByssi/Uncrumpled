

Changing Hotkeys and Viewing Pages
==================================

1. Open the Uncrumpled main User Interface
2. On the left side you will see the icon below. Click it!.

.. image :: img/main_edit_find_resize.png
    :align: center
	
The book viewer will now be loaded on the right half of the screen.

Click on the downward arrow to display the next level of items in the tree.

3. To load a page, navigate to it in the tree and double click the name.

4. To edit Workbook settings click on the workbook name. Change the
Required settings on the right and press save.
